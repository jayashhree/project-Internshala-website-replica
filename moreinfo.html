Want to know more about Internshala?

“Where ambition meets opportunity.”

Internshala is India’s leading platform that connects students with internship opportunities across a wide range of industries. Founded in 2010, Internshala was created with a vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world experience. It empowers students by offering them practical exposure and helping them build valuable professional skills.

Over the years, Internshala has grown into a trusted career-building partner for millions of students and thousands of companies. With internships available in engineering, marketing, content writing, design, data science, and more, it enables students from all backgrounds to find roles that suit their interests and career goals.

Internshala also provides free and affordable online training programs in areas like programming, digital marketing, data analytics, and creative design—making skill-building accessible to everyone. These programs are designed with industry relevance and hands-on learning in mind.

Internshala’s mission is simple: To create a world full of opportunities for students where they can gain experience, confidence, and clarity about their future paths. Today, it has impacted over 10 million students across 100+ cities, helping them take the first step toward successful careers. Internshala continues to expand, innovate, and inspire students to dream bigger and work smarter.
